Some of my story

I am a 90s kid! I was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma in the early 1990s into a loving and supportive family. I grew up enjoying science, cross country, video games, music, and language. FullMoon by Finnish metal band Sonata Arctica is the song that convinced me to pick up the guitar. I used to day dream about dropping out of high school and being in a heavy metal band. (Most days I am glad that this is not what I actually did!) I skipped math homework for two weeks to play Super Smash Bros. Brawl. I went to college as a biochemistry major thinking about maybe being a doctor or going to graduate school. Ultimately I opted for grad school to study organic chemistry. I graduated with my master's degree in 2018 and at the recommendation of a friend I actually ended up applying for a job in corporate client services with proxy advisory firm Institutional Shareholder Services, where I ended up working for nearly 3.5 years. Throughout my life I have tried to expose myself to foreign and ancient languages, including Spanish, Czech, Chinese, Greek, and Latin. I play several video games in Spanish, a couple of my favorites being Skyrim and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. Philosophy did not originally appeal to me, but during graduate school I became more interested in it, particularly in metaphysics. Today, I am learning coding and programming at Coding Temple and hope to become a software and web developer.
