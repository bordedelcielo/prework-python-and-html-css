I am interested in language! My parents bought me a copy of Rosetta Stone Spanish when I was in high school annd I really enjoyed the immersive approach to language. I traveled to China and picked up a bit of Chinese, but my undergrad institution did not offer Chinese courses, so I took French for modern language. For some reason, the French noun I remember most vividly is "poisson" ("fish"). These days I am interested in classical languages, including Latin and Greek. (Salve amicus!) I also continue to study Spanish, and choose to play several video games in Spanish. (Te gusta los juegos!)
I took piano lessons for many years as a child and picked up the guitar in high school. I continue to enjoy music and spend as much time as I can playing my favorite riffs and solos.
